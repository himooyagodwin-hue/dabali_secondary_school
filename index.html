About Dabali Secondary School

Dabali Secondary School is committed to providing quality education that empowers learners with knowledge, discipline and leadership skills. Our school focuses on academic excellence and character development to prepare learners for future opportunities.

We provide a supportive learning environment with dedicated teachers, modern teaching methods and a variety of subjects that help learners develop intellectually, socially and morally.
